about brock

Brock Swinson is a multifaceted creative professional whose journey through the entertainment industry has been marked by a relentless pursuit of storytelling excellence and community building. Born and raised with a passion for narrative arts, Brock's career began in the world of journalism, where he honed his skills as both a writer and editor. His early work managing the Free Bee newspaper and serving as Editor in Chief at Rogue Valley Messenger demonstrated his natural leadership abilities and editorial acumen, skills that would later prove invaluable in his creative endeavors.

Brock's transition into the entertainment industry began with background work on the popular television series One Tree Hill, an experience that provided him with firsthand insight into the collaborative nature of television production. This exposure to the filmmaking process ignited his passion for screenwriting and creative storytelling. His subsequent work on various TV shows, commercials, and music video sets in Los Angeles further deepened his understanding of the industry's technical and creative demands, informing his approach to writing scripts that are both artistically compelling and practically producible.

As a freelance writer and marketing professional, Brock's work with Russell Brunson at ClickFunnels showcased his ability to craft compelling narratives that drive engagement and conversion. This experience in marketing copywriting has directly influenced his screenwriting approach, where he focuses on creating emotionally resonant stories with clear dramatic stakes. His expertise in understanding audience psychology and crafting persuasive content has become a cornerstone of his creative methodology.

Brock's commitment to community building and knowledge sharing is exemplified through his role as co-founder of Buzztown, a thriving screenwriting and filmmaking community. This platform reflects his belief in the power of collaboration and mentorship in creative industries. His podcast and YouTube series, Creative Principles, features interviews with over 200 screenwriters, actors, and directors, providing invaluable insights for aspiring creatives. This extensive research culminated in his debut non-fiction book 'Ink by the Barrel,' which offers practical advice for those seeking careers as prolific writers.

Currently, as the founder of Blue Whistler Films, Brock combines his entrepreneurial spirit with his artistic vision, positioning himself uniquely in the industry as someone who understands both the creative and business aspects of filmmaking. His approach emphasizes authentic storytelling, technical excellence, and community building, recognizing that successful projects require both artistic vision and practical execution. Brock's journey represents the evolution of a creative professional who has successfully bridged the gap between traditional journalism, modern digital marketing, and contemporary filmmaking, all while maintaining a steadfast commitment to helping others achieve their creative goals.
